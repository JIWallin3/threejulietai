Information We Collect

We collect two types of information: Personal Information and Non-Personal Information.

Personal Information includes information that can be used to identify you, such as your name, email address, and age. We collect this information when you create an account, voluntarily share your age and sex with us for personalized responses, or contact us for support.

Non-Personal Information includes data that cannot be used to identify you, such as your browsing history, usage patterns, and aggregated data.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Data collection practices vary depending on the account tier. Free account users agree that all data created or collected while logged in may be submitted for AI analysis and used for personal recommendations and bot recall requirements. Paid account users have greater control over data collection and privacy.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your Personal Information to third parties. We may share Non-Personal Information, such as aggregated user data, with third-party partners for research or analysis purposes.

Our Website is not intended for users under the age of majority in their locale. We do not knowingly collect Personal Information from children. If we become aware that we have collected Personal Information from a child, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.
